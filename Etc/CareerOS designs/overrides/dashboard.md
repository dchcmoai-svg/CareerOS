# Dashboard Override

**Target Route:** `/dashboard` (and `(platform)` general frame)
**Primary Doctrine:** Linear + Raycast

## Core Focus
The dashboard is the **Orchestration Layer** of CareerOS. It must feel like calm, operational intelligence.

## UX Rules & Visual Deviations
- **Orchestration Density:** Information-dense but highly structured. Aggressively filter out low-signal data.
- **Widget Spacing:** Tighter spacing to accommodate dense operational metrics without feeling cluttered.
- **Intelligence Prioritization:** Top opportunities and workflow continuity take precedence over static charts.
- **Notification Placement:** Unobtrusive but highly accessible for quick workflow resolution.
- **AI Panel Behavior:** Contextual Right-Panel Agent acts as the Global Orchestrator, summarizing insights across all systems.
