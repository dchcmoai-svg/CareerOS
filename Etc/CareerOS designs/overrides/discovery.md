# Discovery Override

**Target Route:** `/discovery`
**Primary Doctrine:** Bloomberg Terminal + Linear

## Core Focus
The Discovery feed is the **Retention Engine** and the primary emotional hook after onboarding. It must feel like a "Bloomberg for careers".

## UX Rules & Visual Deviations
- **Signal Density:** Extreme intelligence density. Display what matters instantly.
- **Scan Speed:** Layout must support rapid eye movement and instantaneous opportunity evaluation.
- **Card Hierarchy:** Layered data presentation where primary signals (fit, salary, role) dominate.
- **Ghost Score Visuals:** Represent recruiter responsiveness and stale signals with intuitive, non-anxiety-inducing visuals.
- **Ranking Emphasis:** AI ranking and fit explanations must be prominent and explicitly state *why* a role matches.
- **Keyboard Flow:** Omnipresent keyboard navigation (j/k to move up/down, Enter to expand).
