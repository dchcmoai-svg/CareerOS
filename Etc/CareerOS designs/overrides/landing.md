# Landing Page Override

**Target Route:** `/` (Marketing Ecosystem)
**Primary Doctrine:** Vercel + Apple + Stripe + Arc

## Core Focus
The landing page must deliver **Cinematic Ecosystem Storytelling**. It is not a generic startup hero section. It must convey trust, scale, and intelligence.

## UX Rules & Visual Deviations
- **Cinematic Pacing:** Emotional sequencing and reveal pacing (Apple).
- **GSAP Usage:** Use GSAP for complex scroll-triggered storytelling motion.
- **Ecosystem Reveals:** Staggered orchestration to reveal the interconnected nature of the platform (Arc Browser).
- **Typography Scaling:** Sentence-case headlines, period-terminated, with aggressive negative tracking (`-2.4px` at 48px).
- **Mesh Gradients:** Use the signature multi-stop mesh gradient as atmospheric decoration at hero scale only (Stripe). Do NOT miniaturize it.
