# Resume Lab Override

**Target Route:** `/resume` or `/lab`
**Primary Doctrine:** Notion + Compiler Intelligence

## Core Focus
The Resume Lab is the **Educational and Optimization Engine**.

## UX Rules & Visual Deviations
- **Educational Warmth:** Notion-style readability, maximizing whitespace for focus.
- **ATS Visualizations:** Clear, compiler-like highlighting of "errors" (weak verbs, missing keywords).
- **Inline AI Behavior:** Context-aware fixes that suggest rather than dictate.
- **Focus Mode:** Distraction-free editing environment.
- **Readability:** Warm, legible typography (subtle serifs for previews, geometric sans for the app shell).
