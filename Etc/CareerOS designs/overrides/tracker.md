## Core Focus
The Tracker is the **Operations Engine**. It manages the pipeline of applications, networking, and opportunities flawlessly.

## UX Rules & Visual Deviations
- **Deterministic Transitions:** Every action must feel solid, saved, and trustworthy.
- **Operational Calmness:** Linear's dark canvas and typography hierarchy layered over dense data.
- **Kanban Physics:** Heavy emphasis on satisfying drag-and-drop mechanics.
- **Optimistic Updates:** Instant UI feedback on state changes to maintain workflow speed.
- **Workflow Continuity:** AI follow-up reminders should feel like embedded operational nudges that persist logically.
